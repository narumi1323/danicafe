From Fukuoka and the U.S. to Tokyo

12/30 2023

category:info

This café offers natural food that is good for the body. The menu offers a variety of dishes that feature organic and additive-free ingredients. Heal your body from the inside out with delicious blended coffee and healthy organic food.

Newly opened in Shinkoiwa, Tokyo in the winter of 2023. The owner came to Japan from the U.S. to spread the idea of fast food that is good for the body, and decided to open a new café here.The owner has also studied Japanese café culture in Fukuoka City, a sanctuary for cafés, where he has lived.
